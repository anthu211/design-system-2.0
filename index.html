Resolutions & Breakpoints

Prevalent AI is a desktop-first platform. The layout is optimised for 1440px but supports tablet and mobile viewports in responsive mode.

Primary target: 1440 × 900 and above at 100% browser zoom. QA testing should cover all three viewport categories below.
